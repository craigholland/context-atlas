# Role Projection Template

Use this template when creating `.codex/roles/<role-id>.md`.

## Required Sections

### Purpose

- identify the project role and its accountable scope

### Direct Ownership

- summarize the role's direct artifact, decision, or review ownership

### Parent-Agent Binding

- identify the parent agent that materializes the role

### Mode Participation

- summarize the modes this role commonly enters

### Protocol Participation

- summarize the protocols and review gates the role participates in

### Traceability

- list the upstream authoritative sources
- declare the maintenance mode

## Authoring Rules

- keep this as a role projection, not an agent descriptor
- do not restate the whole accountability matrix verbatim when a short
  projection will do
